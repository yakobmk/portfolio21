Originally from Addis Ababa, Ethiopia. Currently in DC Metropolitan area.

Heyo, I'm Yakob - a designer and developer, hoping to further curate experience and expression in the world of design space.

I was born and raised in Addis Ababa, Ethiopia (oh and by the way I recommend visiting if you haven't been), then after becoming 18 I moved to the US. Because of this, I was able to experience a unique perspective of the world - standing somewhere in the middle of the two ends of the globe led me to view one with the lens of the other and vice versa.

Needless to say that each lifestyles, cultures has its pros and cons. However, what drew my attention and also my exprience was the high volumes of interactions, perspectives, thoughts and preceptions about one another that fall victim to being lost in translation.

As I began to ponder more about this reality, I began to realize the need for finding creative ways to communicate ideas through; the understanding of how large the impact of the digital era could and is providing solutions in this aspect sparked an interest in me towards designing, developing and curating digital platforms, experiences and interactions. In this quest, I graduated from the Boston College, with a B.A. in Computer Science and a focus in Management and Leadership in 2021.

Outside of design, I dance and also play basketball. Favorite team, Miami Heat!

I'm always down to collaborate when I have the time. Reach out and let's chat!
